casper

casper is a Discord bot written in Python with the Discord.py library.

Still a work-in-progress somewhat. It's functional but not ready for public use where I can be hands-off.

It has a variety of features such as, but not limited to:
Commands are broken up into different cogs relatively based on what they do;
games, jukebox, memes, reddit, rpg, news, and utilities
The owner of the bot can use the load and unload commands to manage individual cogs.

FinnTools

FinnTools is a small side project I made that allows a DM to create encounters for their Pathfinder groups.

At the moment, it gives you a very rough idea of what creatures and how many of them would fit into a combat encounter for your group based on the number of players in the group, their levels, and how hard you want the encounter to be. It then gives you a list of creatures that fall into that range.

Created using Python, Flask, and Bootstrap - David Schaeffer 2017
